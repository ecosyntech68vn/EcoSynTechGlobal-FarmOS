# ISMS Policy

Version: 1.0.0
Date: 2026-04-23

Overview
- This ISMS Policy defines the scope, governance, and commitments to information security aligned with ISO/IEC 27001:2022.

Scope
- Applies to EcoSynTech FarmOS platform, including cloud services, on-prem components, software, data, and suppliers involved in the information processing lifecycle.

Policy Statement
- Management is committed to establishing, implementing, maintaining, and continually improving the ISMS to protect information assets.
- All staff and contractors must comply with security controls, risk management, and incident handling as defined in this policy and accompanying procedures.

Roles and Responsibilities
- Top management: approve ISMS scope, allocate resources, review performance.
- ISMS Manager: oversee risk assessment, controls, internal audit, and management reviews.
- All employees and contractors: comply with security controls and report incidents.

Risk Management & Treatment
- Identify, assess, and treat information security risks as per the risk register.
- Maintain a Risk Treatment Plan and a Validated Statement of Applicability.

Compliance & Review
- Conduct annual internal audits and management reviews.
- Align with applicable legal, regulatory, and contractual requirements.

Document Control
- This is a controlled document; revisions must be approved by ISMS Manager.

References
- ISO/IEC 27001:2022
- ISO/IEC 27002:2022 (Annex A references for controls)
