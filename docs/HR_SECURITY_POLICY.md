HR Security Policy (Skeleton)

- Purpose: Define security requirements for human resources and onboarding/offboarding.
- Scope: All employees, contractors, and third parties.
- Roles & Responsibilities: HR, Security, and Managers.
- Hiring: Background checks, NDA, security awareness onboarding.
- Onboarding: Access provisioning, training assignments, device access, data handling.
- Offboarding: Revoke access, collect devices, secure data transfer, data retention compliance.
- Security Awareness & Training: frequency, topics, tracking.
- Acceptable Use Policy: allowed use of systems, incident reporting.
- Access Control for HR systems: least privilege, separation of duties.
- Compliance & Records: retention of HR security training evidence.
- Review and Update: periodic review cadence.
