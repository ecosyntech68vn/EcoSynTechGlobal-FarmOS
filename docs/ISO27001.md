ISO/IEC 27001 alignment (High Level)

Scope
- EcoSynTech Local Core (on-premises) including API, dashboards, device management, data stores, and ML bootstrap components.

Policy and Governance
- Information Security Policy aligned with ISO 27001 requirements.
- Roles & responsibilities defined for security, development, operations, and external interfaces.
- Data classification and handling guidelines.
- Access control policy with RBAC, MFA where applicable, and least privilege.
- Logging, monitoring, and incident response processes.
- Risk assessment and treatment plan maintained as part of the ISMS.

Controls Mapping (example)
- A.9 Access Control: user authentication, authorization, password policy, RBAC.
- A.12 Operational Security: logging, monitoring, protection of logs, change management.
- A.13 Communications Security: data in transit protection, secure API usage.
- A.14 System Acquisition, Development and Maintenance: secure SDLC, vulnerability management.
- A.18 Compliance: legal/regulatory, data retention policy.

Statement of Applicability (SoA)
- Identify applicable controls and status; update as systems evolve.

Risk Management Lifecycle
- Identify, assess, treat, monitor, and review risks on a quarterly basis or as changes occur.

Documentation
- SoA, policy docs, risk register, incident response plan, access control policy, and audit trail definitions are maintained in the repository.
