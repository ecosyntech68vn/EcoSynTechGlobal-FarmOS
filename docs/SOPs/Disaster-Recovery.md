SOP: Disaster Recovery

Goal
- Ensure rapid restore of services after a disaster with minimal data loss.

Scope
- All critical services, data stores, and infrastructure to be covered by DR plan.

RPO/RTO
- Define Recovery Point Objective (RPO) and Recovery Time Objective (RTO) per service.

Recovery Procedures
- Activate DR plan; switch to standby environment or restore data from backups.
- Rebuild services; validate data integrity; run acceptance tests.

Communications
- Notify stakeholders; maintain runbooks.

Maintenance & Testing
- Regular DR tests; update plan after each test.
