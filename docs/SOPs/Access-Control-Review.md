SOP: Access Control Review

Goal
- Ensure consistent application of least privilege and RBAC across the system.

Scope
- Users, groups, roles in API and dashboard access.

Frequency
- Quarterly reviews or after major changes.

Process
- Inventory: List all users and roles.
- Verification: Confirm user-role assignments align with least privilege.
- Adjustments: Revoke unnecessary permissions; reassign roles as needed.
- Evidence: Log review and approvals; update role mappings.

Tools & Evidence
- Access control policy, RBAC mapping, and audit logs.
