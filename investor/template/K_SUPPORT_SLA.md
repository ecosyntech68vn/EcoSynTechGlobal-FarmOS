## 2. PRIORITY DEFINITIONS

### Critical (P1) - 24/7 Response

| Definition | Examples | Action |
|------------|----------|--------|
| System completely down | No data, no alerts | Immediate |
| Data loss | Sensor readings missing | Immediate |
| Security breach | Unauthorized access | Immediate |
| Irrigation failure | Equipment damage risk | Immediate |
| Physical safety | Electrical hazard | Immediate |

### High (P2) - Business Hours

| Definition | Examples | Action |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Major feature broken | Dashboard error | 4 hours |
| Multiple sensors offline | >50% offline | 4 hours |
| Alerts not working | No notifications | 4 hours |
| Data sync failure | Cloud connectivity | 4 hours |
| App crash | Frequent crashes | 4 hours |

### Medium (P3) - Business Hours

| Definition | Examples | Action |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Minor feature broken | Chart error | 24 hours |
| UI/UX issue | Display problem | 24 hours |
| Integration issue | Third-party | 24 hours |
| Performance issue | Slow loading | 24 hours |
| Documentation error | Wrong info | 24 hours |

### Low (P4) - Best Effort

| Definition | Examples | Action |
|------------|----------|--------|
| Feature request | "Add pH alerts" | 1 week |
| Enhancement | "Better charts" | 2 weeks |
| Documentation | Add examples | 2 weeks |
| Translation | Missing VN text | 2 weeks |
| Cosmetic issue | Colors, fonts | 2 weeks |

## 8. SERVICE CREDIT

### SLA Breach Policy

| Breach Type | Credit |
|-----------|--------|
| Response time | 10% monthly credit |
| Resolution time | 25% monthly credit |
| Multiple breaches | 50% monthly credit |
| Critical outage (>4 hours) | 100% month credit |

### Credit Request Process

```
1. Customer reports breach (within 7 days)
2. Support verifies breach
3. Credit calculated
4. Manager approval
5. Credit applied to next invoice
6. Customer notified
```

### Credit Limitations

```
• Maximum credit: 100% of monthly subscription
• Excludes: Hardware, one-time fees
• Requires: Active subscription
• Non-transferable
```
