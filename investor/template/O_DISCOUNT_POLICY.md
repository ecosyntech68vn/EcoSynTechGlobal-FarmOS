## 4. APPROVAL PROCESS

### Discount Request

```
Step 1: Sales rep identifies need
Step 2: Prepare discount justification
Step 3: Submit to manager (with CRM)
Step 4: Manager review (24-48 hours)
Step 5: Finance verification
Step 6: Approval/discount notice
Step 7: Customer communication
```

### Required Documentation

| Discount | Quote | Justification | Approval |
|----------|-------|--------------|----------|
| <5% | ✅ | Email | Auto |
| 5-10% | ✅ | Business case | Manager |

| >20% | ✅ | Board deck | Board |
