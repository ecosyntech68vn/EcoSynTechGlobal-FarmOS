# EcoSynTech FarmOS — Executive Summary / Tóm tắt điều hành

Version: 5.1.0 | Release date: 2026-04-20 | Status: Pilot-ready / Trạng thái: Sẵn sàng cho Pilot

---

Overview / Tổng quan
- EcoSynTech FarmOS là nền tảng nông nghiệp thông minh tích hợp IoT và AI, tuân thủ ISO 27001:2022. Hiện có 27 tài liệu được phân loại rõ ràng (Kỹ thuật, SOP, Nội bộ, Pháp lý, Khách hàng, Audit). Hệ thống đã được thử nghiệm với 39 bài test và sẵn sàng cho Pilot với tối đa 100 ESP32.
- The repo contains deployment assets (Dockerfile, docker-compose.prod.yml, nginx.conf, deploy.sh), SOPs for core operational processes, and formal legal documents (TOS, Privacy Policy, SLA, Service Agreement).

Portfolio Highlights / Điểm nổi bật
- Technical foundation for production: Docker-based deployment, a hardened security posture, and a go-live readiness checklist.
- SOPs: 5 core SOPs covering access control, backups, security incident handling, system startup, and monitoring.
- Legal & Compliance: TOS, Privacy Policy, SLA, Service Agreement, plus ISO 27001 artifacts and audit reports.
- Internal & Customer docs: Employee handbook, internal security policy, incident response plan, data retention policy, acceptable use policy, plus customer onboarding guide.
- Roadmap & Governance: Product roadmap outlining phased releases (Foundation, FarmOS Core, IoT integration, AI assists, traceability, enterprise SaaS) and release governance with Definition of Done and KPI tracking.

Strategic Fit / Phù hợp chiến lược (for Farmer/Coop users)
- 3 pricing tiers (Mini, Basic, Pro) with addons, designed to be affordable for smallholders while enabling HTX scalability.
- Offline-first capabilities and SMS fallbacks to cope with limited rural connectivity.
- Simple, Vietnamese-first UI mode for rapid onboarding and adoption.

Key Metrics / KPI (for business decision-makers)
- Onboarding rate, churn, and ARPU per customer tier
- System uptime, MTTR, and alerting reliability
- Data retention/compliance adherence and audit readiness

Recommended Next Steps / Các bước tiếp theo (high level)
- Finalize HTX-focused MVP features (HTX multi-farm dashboard, 3-node simple UI, ROI calculator) and run a 2-3 month pilot with 2-3 HTX partners.
- Expand addons for Weather and Produce Prices with clear pricing.
- Prepare customer-facing contracts and service SLAs for commercial go-live.
- Publish executive summary to stakeholders and investor-facing materials.
